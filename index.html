Wedding Event

The Details

Call a sitter, leave the moppets at home and come in your 1920's best! Adults only - Great Gatsby style baby! We're bringing this celebration back one-hundred years. All you eggs and flappers that have been invited, join us! RSVP and everything'll be jake. We'll be dipping our bill together, but pace yourself. Don't want to get zazzled right off the bat.

Our celebration will have three parts. In each of these three parts guests are encouraged to laugh, cry, and hold hands with your personal emotional support (aka your guest). All three parts will be hosted at the same location: The Sterling Hotel.

In the event of a raid by the coppers, drink fast.

1
Our Ceremony

It all begins at the Sterling Hotel in downtown Sacramento at 6pm. There is no password for entry. Meet in the ballroom and get your tear-wipes ready.

2
Cocktail Hour

After the ceremony, the bar opens. Migrate yourself to the bar area and ask for a gin rickey. We'll be here for an hour as the reception area is prepared for us.

3
Reception

Now that you're properly sloshed, head back into the ballroom and try your best attempt at the Charleston!

The Sterling

You started the night here, might as well end it here! Here's a quote from their website to convince you to stay here:

"An evening in one of the mansion’s spacious rooms offers you the ambiance of the Victorian era, while pampering you with a luxurious modern comfort."

That sounds nice, don't you think?

There is a room block for our party! Please book using passcode: CC2020.
